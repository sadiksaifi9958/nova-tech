How we use cookies

Last updated: April 2026

What are cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your browsing experience.

How we use cookies

We use cookies to remember your dark mode preference, analyse website traffic, and improve our website performance. We do not use cookies for advertising purposes.

Types of cookies we use

We use functional cookies such as localStorage to remember your theme preference between visits. These are essential for the website to work correctly.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.
